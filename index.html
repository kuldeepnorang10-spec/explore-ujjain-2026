9 Divine Destinations

Every street in Ujjain holds a story. A journey to these sacred places brings peace to the mind and fulfills the soul.

"The Lord of Time, Victory to Shri Mahakal."

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas — the world-famous South-facing lingam. Attending the Bhasma Aarti is a profoundly sacred experience.

"A wonderful blend of ancient tales and modernity."

A beautiful modern corridor featuring intricate murals, stone sculptures, and a serene open layout.

"The guardian of Ujjain, head of the Eight Bhairavas."

The guardian deity of Ujjain. It holds a unique tradition where liquor is offered to the Lord.

"Symbol of strength and devotion from the Maratha era."

An important Shakti Peeth. Famous for its two massive 11th-century lamp pillars where 1008 lamps are lit.

"A spiritual evening on the banks of the sacred Shipra."

The main center of the Kumbh Mela. The daily Shipra Aarti offers eternal peace to the mind.

"Where Lord Narayan himself received education."

Lord Krishna, Balaram, and Sudama received their education in this gurukul. A highly peaceful environment.

"The birthplace of Mars, the son of the Earth."

This red marble temple is considered the birthplace of the planet Mars. Famous for Mangal Dosh Nivaran rituals.

"Residing in three forms, the remover of all worries."

Chintaman, Icchaman, and Siddhivinayak — three idols in one place. Features ancient stone pillars.

"Proof of ancient India's scientific precision."

An 18th-century observatory. Its geometrical structures and giant sundial are prime examples of ancient Indian science.

Pilgrim Assistance

How to Reach Ujjain?

From Indore airport or any major city — our team arranges everything from pickup to Darshan.

By Flight

Nearest airport: Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport, Indore (approx 55 km · 1.5 hrs). Direct AC cabs available from airport. We arrange pickup at INR 1,299 one-way.

By Train

Ujjain Junction (UJN) is well-connected to Delhi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad & Bhopal. Station to temple: 10 minutes. We provide station pickup.

By Road

Volvo buses & AC cabs from Indore (55km) · Bhopal (185km) · Ahmedabad (450km). Direct route to Mahakal Chowk via NH3. We also arrange self-drive cab rentals.

Senior Citizens & Differently-Abled Assistance

Wheelchair arrangements, battery-operated vehicles inside the temple complex, medical kits on all tours, and slow-paced itineraries — we ensure that every devotee, under any condition, can peacefully have Baba Mahakal's darshan.

Bhasma Aarti bookings are done on the official shrine board website (shrimahakaleshwar.com) — slots typically open 30 days in advance. Our team provides complete assistance in confirming your slot, reporting times, dress codes, and overall guidance.

Kaal Sarp Dosh puja is considered highly auspicious around Nag Panchami, but can also be performed on Shivratri and Mondays. For Mangal Dosh, Tuesday is the best day. Our team will suggest the ideal time based on your astrological chart.
